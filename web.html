Summary of Web Portals Features

Our member web portals are designed for both debt and equity depositors and/or shareholders (mortgage pool shareholders or any kind bank or credit union depositors). Above are screenshots of our starting templates along a couple example images of the finished product.

These pages are configurable with your artwork and branding along with any documents you would like to display. Once set up our software will then update your web portal data every few minutes.

  • Account Summary Page - this is the page your member will first see upon login
  • Account Detail Page - for each debt or equity account they will have one of these screens
  • Feedback Form Page - allows your members to text/message you from their web portal
  • Member Account Details Page - shows your members their account info
